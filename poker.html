How to Play

Poker is a popular card game where players bet on the strength of their hand. Heres how to play

1. Place your bet and click "Deal Cards".

2. You will be dealt five cards.

3. Choose which cards to keep and which to discard.

4. Click "Draw Card" to replace the discarded cards.

5. Your final hand will be evaluated, and winnings will be awarded based on the hand strength.
